Overview

Problem Description

Procrastination is a common problems that can have negative effects once out of hand. The constant struggle to meet deadlines, and lack of time to finish tasks can cause be overwhelming at times.
We wanted to design an application that could help the public alleviate this problem, and develop time management skills, in an entertaining way.
Proposal

User Research

In our research phase, we initially began by interviewing multiple potential users for their opinions on procrastination, and their own methods with dealing with it. For consistency we developed a script that would be read to each interviewee, which consisted of both warm-up questions and questions that focused on extracting specific information on the subject of procrastination and its effects.

Final Design

Based on the user research and the personas we created, we turn that into epics. With these epics, we can really hone in on what features our users wants. From each epic, we can create user stories detailing the actions that a user would do with this feature. After we completed that, we begin a figma workspace in order to create a wireframe for our system. Every feature that was created is the direct result of the epics and stories that we created from our user research. User Epics and Stories
