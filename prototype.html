Prototyping

In the wireframe we constructed, we initially had our user's character profile a the initial screen they saw when fist logging in. We also decided that the best way to navigate through our application would be through a navigation bar. Through this, the user would be able to access their calendar and agenda there. In our wireframe we thought it would be beneficial to include the task creation page, to gauge whether the pacing and process was intuitive.
