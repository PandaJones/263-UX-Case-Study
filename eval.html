Evaluation Results

Upon a second round of interviews, we created a revised wireframe. Many of the comments and suggestions we were able to gather from our subjects focused on the lack of navigation buttons on some screens, and the lack of detail and/or clarification with viewing and adding tasks to the agenda. We therefore, strove to add user-friendly buttons and included fake inputs into some of the fields to better give users an idea of what would be expected from them when using our features.
